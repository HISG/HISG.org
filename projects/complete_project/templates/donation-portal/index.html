Reach out to your Worldwide Neighbors: Contribute Resources To Meet the Needs

The lives of our neighbors all over the world are impacted daily by natural disasters. Whether it is a Tsunami (Indonesia), Earthquake (Pakistan) or Hurricane (U.S. Gulf Coast), lives are often changed in an instant. Can you help us make a difference? Your tax deductible donation will reach out and touch those in need. Your gift can provide for general disaster response, humanitarian aid & relief effort or a development project of your choice. We have trusted friends, associates and counterparts throughout the world that will commit your gift to its most beneficial use.
