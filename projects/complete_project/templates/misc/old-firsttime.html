Humanitarian is simply another word for caring

Humanitarian International Services Group (HISG) has worked to bring hope and help to suffering communities in more than 80 countries around the world. Through disaster relief operations, community development projects, gift-in-kind shipments and volunteer service, we are making a difference by connecting resources to those in great need. We invite you to join with us in touching lives of men, women and children, both at home and abroad.

The information on our website is divided into five major categories

  • News and Photos
  • Projects and Models
  • Featured Initiatives
  • About HISG
  • Donation Portal
You can watch this video introducing each section or read about them below


In News and Photos, you can read the latest updates from HISG's relief and development projects and browse photo galleries from around the world.


HISG's Featured Initiatives are the overarching strategic efforts that drive what HISG is doing. There is and initiative for Orphan Care; the Hope Resource Network, which manages gift-in-kind donations, the first of its kind Global Information Center; the much talked-about Let's Save One campaign, and the Starfish Community.


In About HISG, you can read about our organization's international offices, background, purpose, mission and vision; find staff biographies and pictures; and contact us via email.


Our Donation Portal is for people who have partnered with us financially and for those who would like to do so. It offers a secure and convenient method of giving through HISG to serve those in need. Donors can direct where their money goes, can read the latest information from projects they support, and view printable donation records.
